## 🎯 Dashboard Objective

Provide interactive insights into **what drives cereal health ratings** for product developers, nutritionists, and business stakeholders.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Cereals Analyzed
- Average Health Rating
- Average Calories per Serving
- Cold vs Hot Cereals

### Key Visualizations

1. **Rating Distribution** – Histogram + Box Plot
2. **Sugars vs Rating** – Scatter plot (strongest negative driver)
3. **Protein vs Rating** – Scatter plot
4. **Calories vs Rating** – Scatter plot
5. **Average Rating by Manufacturer** – Bar Chart
6. **Rating Distribution by Manufacturer** – Box Plot
7. **Nutrient Correlation Heatmap**
8. **Shelf Position Analysis**

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

- **Sugar is the #1 enemy** of high cereal ratings (strong negative correlation)
- Higher **protein** and **fiber** content significantly improve ratings
- Some manufacturers consistently outperform others in health ratings
- Lower calorie cereals tend to receive higher ratings
- The top 3 nutrients (`sugars`, `protein`, `fat`) can predict ~79% of rating variation

---

## 💡 Recommendations

1. **Reformulate high-sugar cereals** to improve health scores
2. **Emphasize protein** in marketing and product development
3. **Target lower calorie + higher fiber** combinations
4. Use the predictive model to estimate ratings for new cereal concepts

---

## 🛠️ How to Use the Dashboard

- Open `Cereal_Rating_Dashboard.pbix` in **Power BI Desktop**
- Use slicers to filter by Manufacturer, Shelf, or Type
- Hover over visuals for detailed tooltips
- All charts are fully interactive
